ZINC PHOSPHATE

ECOPHOS-710

Zinc Phosphate for Immersion Lines

Concentrated liquid designed to produce a fine-grain zinc phosphate coating resistant to corrosion. Contains Nickel for enhanced protection. Compatible with carbon steel, galvanized sheet, and aluminum.

Immersion Multi-substrate Nickel-enhanced
Request Quote Download TDS

Key Features

  • Fine-grain zinc phosphate coating for superior finish
  • High corrosion resistance with nickel enhancement
  • Multi-substrate compatibility (steel, galvanized, aluminum)
  • Compatible with Ecolkem cleaning and degreasing products
  • Suitable for immersion tank operations
  • Concentrated liquid for economical use

Recommended Applications

ECOPHOS-710 is ideal for automotive components, metal fabrication, appliances, hardware manufacturing, and general metal finishing operations where superior corrosion protection is required. Particularly effective for parts that will be powder coated or painted, as the zinc phosphate layer provides excellent adhesion for subsequent coatings. Recommended for batch processing in immersion tanks with controlled temperature and agitation.

Application Guide

1

Surface Cleaning

Clean parts thoroughly using an Ecolkem degreaser (ECOCLEAN series) to remove oils, grease, and contaminants. Rinse with clean water.

2

Surface Conditioning

Apply titanium-based surface conditioner (ECOPREP series) by immersion for 30-60 seconds at ambient temperature to promote fine-grain crystal formation.

3

Phosphating

Immerse parts in ECOPHOS-710 bath at 45-55°C for 3-5 minutes. Maintain total acidity at 18-25 points and free acidity at 0.8-1.5 points.

4

Water Rinse

Rinse parts with clean water to remove residual chemicals. Use fresh water overflow rinse for best results.

5

Sealing Rinse

Apply ECOSEAL-810 chromium-free sealer to improve corrosion resistance and paint adhesion. Immerse for 30-60 seconds.

6

Drying

Dry parts thoroughly using forced air or oven at 100-120°C. Proceed with powder coating or painting as soon as possible after drying.

Safety Information

Warning

This product is acidic. Always wear appropriate personal protective equipment (PPE) when handling.

  • Wear safety goggles and chemical-resistant gloves
  • Use in well-ventilated area or with local exhaust ventilation
  • Avoid contact with skin and eyes
  • In case of eye contact, flush immediately with water for 15 minutes
  • Refer to SDS for complete safety information

Storage Information

Storage Temperature

Store between 5°C and 40°C (41°F - 104°F). Do not freeze.

Containers

Keep in original, tightly closed containers. Use polyethylene or stainless steel containers only.

Shelf Life

12 months from date of manufacture when stored as recommended.

Disposal

Dispose of in accordance with local, state, and federal regulations. Do not discharge into drains or waterways.
