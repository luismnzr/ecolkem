Deoxidizers

Our deoxidizers are formulated to effectively remove rust, scale, and oxides from metal surfaces, restoring them to a clean, bright finish ready for further processing or coating.

Carbon Steel

Acid-based deoxidizers for removing rust and mill scale from carbon steel and cast iron. Available with inhibitors to prevent over-etching.

Stainless Steel & Aluminum

Specialized formulations for removing heat tint, discoloration, and oxides from stainless steel and aluminum without damaging the base metal.

Copper & Alloys

Gentle formulations for copper, brass, and bronze that remove tarnish and oxides while brightening the surface.

Corrosion Inhibitors

Our corrosion inhibitors provide temporary protection for metal parts during in-process storage or short transit periods. They eliminate the need for oil-based protectants, reducing costs and simplifying downstream processing.

For Carbon Steel

  • Water-based rust preventive coatings
  • Short-term protection (days to weeks)
  • Compatible with painting and coating processes
  • No oily residue to remove

For Copper & Alloys

  • Anti-tarnish treatments for copper and brass
  • Preserves surface brightness
  • Thin film, easy to apply
  • Suitable for storage and short transit

Why Use Water-Based Inhibitors?

No need for oil-based protectants or their removal
Reduced operating costs and processing steps
Environmentally friendly, water-based formulations
