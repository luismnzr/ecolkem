About Ecolkem

Our Story

Founded in Monterrey, Mexico, Ecolkem has been at the forefront of industrial chemical manufacturing for over 25 years. We specialize in developing and delivering high-performance chemical solutions for metalworking, surface treatment, graphic arts, and industrial maintenance industries across North America.

Our dual presence—Ecolkem S.A. de C.V. managing Mexican operations and Ecolkem International, LLC serving US and export markets—allows us to provide seamless support to customers on both sides of the border. This strategic positioning ensures reliable supply chains, local technical expertise, and responsive customer service.

At Ecolkem, we believe that innovation, quality, and sustainability are not just goals—they're responsibilities. Our team of chemists and engineers work closely with customers to develop custom formulations that meet specific operational needs while minimizing environmental impact. From research and development to production and technical support, we're committed to helping our clients achieve optimal performance in their processes.

Today, Ecolkem serves hundreds of industrial facilities across manufacturing sectors, earning trust through consistent product quality, technical expertise, and a genuine partnership approach to solving complex chemical challenges.
