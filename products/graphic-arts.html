Flexography Cleaners

Our flexography cleaning products are designed to maintain optimal print quality while being safe for operators and the environment.

CLEANILOX

Anilox Roller Cleaner

Highly effective cleaner for ceramic and chrome anilox rollers. Removes dried ink, varnish, and adhesive buildup from cells without damaging the roller surface.

  • Non-flammable
  • Low solvent content
  • Safe for ceramic and chrome rollers

FLEXOMATE

Photopolymer Plate Cleaner

Ink remover specially formulated for cleaning photopolymer plates used in flexographic printing. Removes ink residue without swelling or damaging the plate.

  • Non-flammable
  • Plate-safe formula
  • Extends plate life

Offset Cleaners

Specialized cleaning solutions for offset printing equipment, designed to maintain print quality and extend equipment life.

ECOSOLVE-E7

Roller Cleaner

High-performance cleaner for offset ink rollers. Effectively removes conventional and UV inks with low VOC content. Non-flammable and safe for rubber rollers.

PLAX-400

Plate & Blanket Cleaner

Multi-purpose cleaner for offset plates and blankets. Removes ink buildup, paper lint, and glazing. Compatible with conventional and UV-cured inks.

General Products

Additional products for general use in printing facilities.

ECOSOLVE-E5

Rubber Remover / Dielectric Solvent

Versatile solvent for removing rubber and adhesive residues from equipment. Also serves as a safety dielectric solvent for electrical cleaning. Non-flammable.

ECOCREAM-INK

Hand Cleaner Cream

Specially formulated hand cleaner for press operators. Effectively removes printing inks, grease, and grime while being gentle on skin. Contains moisturizers and conditioners.

Safety First

All our graphic arts products are formulated with low solvent content and are non-flammable, ensuring a safer working environment for press operators while meeting environmental regulations.
