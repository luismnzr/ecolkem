Our Development Process

We follow a structured, 6-step process to ensure every development project delivers measurable results and meets your specific requirements.

1

Problem Definition

We work with you to clearly define the problem, identify requirements, constraints, and desired outcomes. This includes understanding your current process, materials, and quality standards.

2

Alternative Search

Our team of chemists and engineers research potential solutions, leveraging our extensive experience and technical databases to identify the most promising approaches.

3

Potential Benefits Estimation

We estimate the technical and economic benefits of each alternative, including cost savings, performance improvements, and environmental impact reductions.

4

Lab Work

Using our fully equipped R&D laboratory, we develop and test prototype formulations. Multiple iterations ensure the product meets all performance criteria before moving to pilot scale.

5

Pilot Tests

We conduct pilot-scale tests at your facility or in our labs under real-world conditions to validate performance and fine-tune the formulation before full-scale production.

6

Results Delivery

We deliver a comprehensive report with test results, product specifications, and implementation recommendations. Full technical support is provided during rollout.
